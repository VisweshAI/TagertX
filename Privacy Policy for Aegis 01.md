# Privacy Policy for Aegis 01

**Effective Date:** March 24, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 24, 2026

## Introduction

Aegis 01 ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our medication safety tracking application.

## Key Principle: Your Data Stays on Your Device

Aegis 01 is designed with privacy as a core principle. **All medication data is stored locally on your device.** We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to your personal health information.

## Information We Collect

### Local Data Storage
The following information is stored **only on your device** using local storage:
- Medication names and details
- Ingredient lists
- Dosage and frequency information
- Your health profile (allergies, medical conditions, age, pregnancy status)
- Medication alerts and warnings
- App usage preferences

**This data never leaves your device** and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

### Images and Label Scanning
When you use the camera to scan medication labels:
- Images are captured on your device
- Images are sent to our server for processing only
- The image is analyzed using AI to extract ingredients
- **The image is deleted from our server immediately after processing**
- We do not store, archive, or retain any images
- We do not use images for any purpose other than ingredient extraction

### API Calls
When checking drug interactions:
- We query the FDA's RxNorm database for official drug interaction data
- Your medication names are sent to RxNorm API to check for interactions
- RxNorm does not identify you personally—only the drug names are transmitted
- We do not track which medications you query

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect:
- Your name, email address, or phone number
- Your location or device identifier
- Your medical history beyond what you voluntarily enter
- Your app usage patterns or analytics
- Your device information or advertising ID
- Cookies or tracking technologies
- Any biometric data

## Permissions and How We Use Them

### Camera Permission
- **Purpose:** To scan medication labels
- **Usage:** Only when you tap the scan button
- **Data Handling:** Images are processed and deleted immediately

### Photo Library Permission (Optional)
- **Purpose:** To select existing medication photos
- **Usage:** Only when you choose to select a photo
- **Data Handling:** Photos are processed and deleted immediately

### Notification Permission
- **Purpose:** To alert you of severe drug interactions
- **Usage:** Only to send notifications about your medications
- **Data Handling:** Notifications are generated locally on your device

## Third-Party Services

### FDA RxNorm API
We use the FDA's RxNorm API to check for official drug interactions. When you check for interactions:
- Your medication names are sent to the RxNorm API
- RxNorm does not identify you personally
- RxNorm does not track your queries
- See [RxNorm Privacy](https://www.nlm.nih.gov/research/umls/rxnorm/) for more information

### No Other Third Parties
We do not share your data with:
- Advertisers or marketing companies
- Analytics services
- Data brokers
- Social media platforms
- Any other third parties

## Data Security

Your local data is protected by:
- Your device's built-in security features
- Local encryption provided by your operating system
- No transmission of sensitive data over the internet
- No cloud storage or remote servers storing your health information

## Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:
- **View:** All your data is visible within the app
- **Edit:** You can edit or delete any medication or health information
- **Delete:** You can delete the app and all associated data at any time
- **Export:** You can manually export your medication list (future feature)
- **No Account:** No account creation means no account data to manage

## Data Retention

- **Local Data:** Stored on your device indefinitely until you delete it
- **Processed Images:** Deleted from our servers immediately after processing
- **API Queries:** Not logged or retained by us

## Children's Privacy

Aegis 01 is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us immediately.
